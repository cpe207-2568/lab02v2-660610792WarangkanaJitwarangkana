My Experience

Choosing Computer Engineering has been a rollercoaster, balancing genuine enjoyment with demanding challenges. I find the intellectual stimulation and the "aha!" moments of solving complex problems truly fun and rewarding. However, the heavy workloads and frustrating debugging sessions can be mentally draining, leading to moments of doubt. Despite the difficulties, the field's vast opportunities and the constant demand for logical thinking and problem-solving skills make me cautiously optimistic about its potential for a relevant and impactful future. It's a journey of mixed feelings, but one that's building a solid foundation.
